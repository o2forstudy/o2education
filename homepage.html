FAQs

Who is O2 Education?

O2 Education & Immigration Pvt. Ltd. is one of the most reputed and trusted name in education and immigration consultancy in Gujarat. Established in 2013, we have successfully guided thousands of students towards achieving their dreams of studying abroad or excelling in competitive exams in India.

What is our goal?

Our goal is to provide quality education and immigration services to students and professionals who want to study or work abroad. We also provide training for various competitive exams in India.

Who is the Owner of O2 Education?

Dr. Arpit Trivedi is the Owner of O2 Education.

Where is the office located?

Our office is located at Vrundavan Square, T.B. Cross Road, Patan, Gujarat.

Which countries do you provide study visa guidance for?

We assist students interested in studying in countries such as Canada, the UK, Australia, New Zealand, the USA and other popular study destinations.

How long does IELTS and PTE coaching take?

Course duration varies according to the student's current English proficiency and target score.

Do you help with university and college admissions?

Yes, We guide students in selecting suitable institution, preparing application, and completing admission procedures.

How can I book a consultation?

You can contact our office directly by phone or visit our Patan office for a counseling session.

Why should students choose O2 Education & Immigration?

We focus on personalized guidance, exam preparation, admission support, and visa assistance to help students achieve their international education goals.

What are your working hours?

The education center generally operates from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Monday to Saturday.

How can I take admission at O2 Education?

Taking admission is simple. Visit our office with your academic documents and identification proof, meet with our counselor to discuss your goals, choose the appropriate course or service, complete the registration process, and to submit the required fees. Our team will guide you through each step and helpyou get started with your classes or study abroad journey.
